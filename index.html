Aadhaar SafeGuard

Protecting Aadhaar Data Through Awareness & Prevention

Why Aadhaar Safety Matters

Aadhaar is one of the most important identity documents in India. Due to lack of awareness, many citizens fall victim to data misuse, identity theft, and financial fraud.

Common Aadhaar Scams

  • Fake calls asking for OTP
  • Phishing messages and fake links
  • Unauthorized Aadhaar usage

Safety Tips

  • Never share OTP with anyone
  • Share Aadhaar only with trusted sources
  • Use masked Aadhaar whenever possible
